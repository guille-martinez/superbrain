# Daily Logistics News

Daily recap of logistics industry news delivered via email.

## Configuration

| Field       | Value                                    |
|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| Model       | Claude Sonnet 4.6                        |
| Trigger     | Daily at 7:00 AM GMT+2                   |
| Connector   | Gmail                                    |
| Recipient   | guillermo.martinez@opereit.ai            |

## What it does

- Scans for relevant logistics industry news
- Explains key concepts and technical/industry-specific terms in plain English
- Prioritizes stories with real business impact over minor updates
- Keeps a professional but easy-to-read tone
- Includes a link per story
- Sends the recap via email
